   Claim My Space

Use this form to claim or release your office space.

   Queue

The Queue site provides a way for visitors to sign in when they walk in to the Registrar's Office. At the moment of sign-in, the site captures the visitor's personal information from the Student Information System. Staff members are empowered to enter additional records of interactions with students and their families. Also, flags can be added to personal records to alert future staff members of special notes or out-of-the-ordinary situations. This site houses ongoing and comprehensive data about each student's interaction with our office.

   Documentation

The Documentation site holds internal information for staff members of the Registrar's Office. Instructions, descriptions, definitions, procedures, and policies are available for all areas within the office. Because each information item is organized within menus as card as by tagging, staff members can find what they need quickly and through various paths. An advanced search function ensures all information on any topic is easily retrievable. Users must log-in. Only Registrar's Office staff are allowed entry.

   Honor Cords

The Honor Cords site coordinates the distribution of honor cords in the days prior to a commencement ceremony. Tentative honors at graduation (recognized at Commencement) are determined by the grade-point average (GPA) entering the graduating term and the sum total of graded hours accumulated prior to the graduation term plus graded hours registered for and pending during the graduation term.

   Physical Inventory

The Physical Inventory site keeps an up-to-date accounting of all significant technological items in possession of the Division of Enrollment Services, including the components of each employee's workstation (desktop computer, monitor, etc.) It also facilitates the check-out process for borrowable items (laptops, tablets, etc.) with an electronic version of the Institute's Equipment Loan Agreement Form (ELA), which managers can approve or reject online.

   Query Library

The Query Library site organizes the SQL scripts that are essential to Registrar's Office business practices. Some queries select specific populations of students who then receive tailored communications and/or have their records altered in some way. Some queries select certain types of courses that fit given parameters related to curriculum governance and/or academic scheduling. Some queries pull data which make other queries possible.
